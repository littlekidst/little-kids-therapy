How it works

From your first call to your child's first breakthrough.

A simple, transparent process. No confusing forms to start — just a friendly conversation, clear next steps, and support from a real person.

01

Initial call

Tell us about your child. We'll listen, answer questions, and walk through what to expect — no pressure and no sensitive details needed in the web form.

02

BCBA assessment

Our BCBA does a comprehensive evaluation to understand your child's strengths and target the right goals for the next 6 months.

03

Plan the next step

After the call, we guide you through the safest way to share documents and decide whether home, clinic, or a hybrid schedule fits best.

04

Therapy begins

Your child's RBT begins regular sessions at home, in our clinic, or both — with weekly BCBA supervision built in.

05

Parent partnership

We meet with you regularly to share progress, train you on strategies, and adjust the plan as your child grows.

06

Ongoing growth

As skills generalize and goals are met, we celebrate every win — and prepare your child for what's next.
